About

Hello There! My name is Steven Cadick. I am a student with big dreams. Dreams that will allow me to succeed and flourish in the industry that I have chosen. I am an up and coming Motion Design Artist. My Portfolio represents that with the content I upload. My hope is that you will see the passion that I have through content I upload. My work is unlike any other. I am unique which gives me a great quality and a different outlook than most other people have.

I like to watch as many movies as I can and see how directors and producers edit their films. It inspires me and helps me creatively when making videos of my own. Some of my favorite films include; Lucky Number Slevin, Reality Bites, The Departed, Training Day and Edward Scissorhands. Take a look around and enjoy what I have to offer.
